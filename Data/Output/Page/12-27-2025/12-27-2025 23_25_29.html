Grow Savings 

A simple way to grow your savings

Sometimes the best way to save is to keep it simple. With a Grow Savings Account, there are no hassles and no frills. Whether you’re saving for retirement, a vacation, or future tuition costs, this is a great account to help your savings grow.

  • Monthly maintenance fee of $3 per month

  • No monthly maintenance fee with a $200 minimum daily balance

  • No monthly maintenance fee when you complete a monthly transfer from a Columbia personal deposit account to your Grow Savings account via online/mobile banking, telephone banking, or an automatic transfer

  • No monthly maintenance fee for primary account holders who are 18 and younger or 55 and older

  • Pinnacle Checking account holders receive up to five Thrive Money Market accounts with no monthly maintenance fee

  • Visit veteransbenefitsbanking.org for more information and resources.

Start saving today!

Grow Savings Account Details

Monthly maintenance fee options:

  • $3
  • $0 when you keep a $200 minimum daily balance
  • $0 when the primary account holder is 18 and younger or 55 and older
  • $0 when you complete a monthly transfer from an Columbia personal deposit account to your Grow Savings account. Qualifying transfers can be made via online/mobile banking, telephone banking or scheduled as an automatic transfer.
  • $0 for up to three Grow Savings accounts when linked to an Access Checking account
  • $0 for up to five Grow Savings accounts when linked to Pinnacle Checking account
  • $0 if military benefit applies (see below)
  • All other fees apply.

Balance requirements are calculated three business days before the end of the deposit statement period.

Military Benefit


Columbia Bank is a proud partner of the Veterans Benefits Banking Program. Our Grow Savings has no monthly maintenance fee for active-duty service members, veterans, their beneficiaries and their caregivers.

 

Withdrawals and transfers:


This account allows you six total withdrawals and transfers each calendar month or statement cycle. Withdrawals and transfers include, but are not limited to: bill payments, telephone transfers, ACH payments, drafts and point of sale transactions. Excess transaction fees apply if limit is exceeded. Unlimited complimentary Umpqua ATM and in-branch withdrawals.

 

Checks


No checks issued or allowed for withdrawal against savings accounts. Any checks, including over the counter checks and electronic checks issued against the savings accounts will be returned unpaid.
